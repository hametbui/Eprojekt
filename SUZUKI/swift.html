UPGRADE THEIR STYLE

Beauty is not only expressed through the appearance, it is important to the core values ​​inside. Swift is a car "born to enjoy driving". Powerful appearance, compact outside, spacious inside. Stand out thanks to the dynamic design combined with the embossed ribs on the body. The overall appearance of Swift creates a feeling of movement even when standing still, increasing the driver's emotions.

The instrument cluster is easy to see and stylish

The two main clocks are elegantly rimmed with chrome, the outstanding features are shown in red and white. Emphasize the sporty, easy-to-see design with the main instrument cluster displaying temperature and fuel level, giving it the style of a true sports watch. The multi-information LCD display is located in the center of the instrument cluster to inform the driver of accurate and easy-to-understand data about the vehicle's status and operation.

Comfortable, sporty interior

The new feature in the interior compartment with the main silver-plated decoration color on the black background makes the New Swift 2021 interior compartment fresh and outstanding, more elegant and no less sophisticated. Besides, the clock cluster has a stylish and personality design with outstanding red and white colors, the information is arranged scientifically to help users easily observe. In addition, the push-button start mode, the sporty D-shaped steering wheel with nodding and the driver's seat with height adjustment are also valuable details, bringing comfort to the user.

Lighter and stronger

“HEARTECT” is a new generation of high-rigidity chassis that contributes to increased crash safety, thanks to the continuously curved shape of the chassis with fewer joints for better energy dispersion. volume in the event of a collision. The lighter chassis incorporates the use of lightweight, high-strength steel to enhance engine performance when running, turning and stopping, as well as increasing fuel economy. As part of “HEARTECT”, the newly designed front and rear systems provide a powerful and emotional driving experience, authentic feel and smoother, more comfortable ride in the Swift.
