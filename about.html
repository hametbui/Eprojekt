ABOUT

Carrio Motor Cars is a family owned dealership established in 1987 based out of sunny South Florida! We have specialized in handpicked, one of a kind pre-owned vehicles! We have the cars, we have the years of knowledge and experience and now, with our new and renovated online shopping tool, your dream car can be delivered to you in just a few simple steps. We have gained our Top Seller reputation on our Ebay Channel by outselling all dealers in our market with unbeatable prices and the best quality inventory! And in the event that you can't find your dream car in our inventory, you can tell us specifically what you want and we will have our experts find the perfect car for you! If you're looking to finance, we work with banks and credit unions to get you the best interest rate possible! Simply fill out the credit application and our credit analyst will contact you shortly to review the best financing options for you! We like to provide as many pictures of our vehicles as possible as well as video footage and a very detailed description for the many buyers that we attend to thousands of miles away from us, so even if you're located in California or Washington State, we're here to help! Our customer feedback speaks louder than anything we can say so we invite you to review our testimonials page to see our customers experience and trust in us to become your dealer of choice! Feel free to call us at any time and it would be our pleasure to assist you in every step of your next vehicle purchase!
