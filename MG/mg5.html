MORE THAN FACILITIES

MG5 owns a spacious space, the interior compartment is designed in a sporty style with a 6-way electric driver's seat. The sunroof and luxuriously designed taplo with diamond motifs bring you interesting moments. A 10'' touch screen entertainment screen that connects Apple CarPlay and Android Auto combined with a multi-function steering wheel that integrates 3 driving modes and a 7'' virtual screen helps create a new look for the interior.

MORE THAN OPERATION

Discover a new driving experience with a 1.5L engine and a CVT gearbox that produces a maximum capacity of 112Hp and a maximum torque of 150Nm. New engineering has been improved to increase ground clearance and body size, bringing strong vitality and sporty style to the MG5. The 3-way power steering steering wheel and control panel are designed to best suit the driver to fully experience the true sporty driving feeling.

MORE THAN SAFETY

SAFETY EXCEEDED with MG's synchronous protection system, a safety system with European technology integrated with features such as Blind Spot Warning, Lane Change Assist, Cross Traffic Alert Rear, Rear Collision Warning, Disc brake drying system, MacPherson front suspension and torsion bar rear suspension help increase rigidity, providing solid safety. In addition, the MG5 is equipped with a 6-airbag system and a 360-degree 3D display camera to help you move safely on any road.
